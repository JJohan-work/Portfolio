About Me

This section is unfinished and will be expanded later

History

I was born in Bend Oregon and moved to Portland Oregon several years ago. I have always been fascinated by the electronic and mechanical fields. I have been coding in some shape or form for around 5 years however only in a self-study and hobby form. Most of my experience is with hardware and low level logic and I hope to eventually get a job in designing computer devices and hardware.

Why I am at Epicodus

While I have been learning about programming on my own I do not have any certifications or have taken any higher education CS degrees. I am hoping Epicodus can be a first step into the professional world of computers and computer design.

My Skills

Profesionally I have worked as an: electrician, carpenter and building construction. In my own time Ive done extensive work on car mechanics and building simple computers and electronic devices however I have a long way to go before I can consider selling those skills

My Learning Goals

My long term goal is to continue to expand my knowledge on computer science. I'd like to contine to become more comfortable with HTML,CSS and bootstrap for quick deployment of usable sites.
